ABOUT

Brian Lionardi was born in Jakarta, 9 November 2006. Without any musical talent from the family, Brian got his own talent and way to develop his talent in music. Brian Lionardi has been studying music since 2016. He got his first music lessons with the keyboard in the school's music extracurricular. Brian can easily learn and understand. From that time, Brian's family providing their support by purchasing his first keyboard. After the development of Brian's music talent, he bought a new guitar in 2019. He learned guitar by self-taught and got a rapid development.

With 7 years of education and experience, Brian Lionardi has been performing in majors event in Harapan Indah and its environs. Brian Lionardi winned some competition in Yamaha. Brian Lionardi is best known for his piano and guitar skills.

Besides his talent in music, Brian has a good ability in math and other subjects related to science at school. At Junior High School, he received many awards from the highest grade. He also likes sports, especially badminton. He has been playing badminton since 2018. Now he is joining as an active player at badminton club.
